Who are we?

Georgian Scooters National Federation is membership based non-commercial legal entity. It was created for development of scooters industry in Georgia which includes:

  • Scooters
  • Electric Scooters
  • Snow scooters

    • Another reason of its creation is reducing of air pollution in Georgia with the development of eco-friendly transport – scooters.
